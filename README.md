# GFTR

*A social gifting and reminder app to plan, organize, and send meaningful gifts.*

---

## Overview

**GFTR** is a Flutter-based social gifting platform designed to help users remember special occasions, plan thoughtful gifts, and stay connected with friends.
With built-in reminders, friend management, messaging, and gift discovery, GFTR makes it easier to celebrate the people you care about.

From birthdays and anniversaries to personal milestones, GFTR ensures you never forget an important moment.
